Here is a simple calculating machine. It stacks the boxes until a box with an operating sign (+,-,* or /) appears. Then this operation is carried out with the two underlying boxes.

Try these examples:

  • 2+3 must be entered as 2 3 +
  • 10-2 must be entered as 10 2 -
  • 5*2+3 must be entered as 5 2 * 3 +
  • 5+2*3 must be entered as 5 2 3 * +
  • (8-2)*(3+4) must be entered as 8 2 - 3 4 + *

How must the following computation be entered: 4*(8+3)-2?
